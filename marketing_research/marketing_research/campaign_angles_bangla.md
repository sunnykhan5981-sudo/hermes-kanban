## Campaign Narratives

### Narrative 1: "এক তরুণের যাত্রা"
- Day 1: discovers LUDOLAND (curiosity)
- Day 7: learns basics (tutorial hook)
- Day 14: first win (social proof)
- Day 30: earning regularly (testimonial)
- Day 60: refers ১০ বন্ধু (community loop)

### Narrative 2: "বিশ্বাস গড়ন"
- Problem: Trust deficit in BD gaming apps
- Process: Show payment proofs, BD faces, local office
- Proof: Live withdrawal, customer testimonial, news feature
- Result: " Biswas এভারী যেখানে"

### Narrative 3: "পরিবার খেলে"
- Hook: Mothers, fathers, uncles playing together
- Story: Family bonding through LUDOLAND
- Event: Family tournament Sundays
- CTA: "আজ বাড়ির সবাই খেলো!"
