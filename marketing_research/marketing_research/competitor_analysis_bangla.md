## Gaps & Opportunities (LUDOLAND Advantage)

### Underserved Niches
1. **Pure Bangla content**: Competitors 70%+ English/Hindi
2. **Female gamers**: Only 15% of competitor ads feature women
3. **Rural Bangladesh**: Minimal targeting beyond Dhaka
4. **Trust/content**: Slow payment proof rare in ads
5. **Festival tie-ins**: Competitors miss Eid/Puja windows

### Unique Angles
- "বাংলাদেশির安卓"— Local-first positioning
- "শিশুদের শিখা"— Educational angle for parents
- "গ্রামীন.connect"— Rural connectivity focus
- "এক পরিবার"— Family gaming angle

### Policy Risks to Avoid
- No guaranteed income claims
- No "withdraw" in ads (use "Redeem")
- No before/after balance as guarantee
- No celebrity endorsement without disclosure
- No targeting minors (<18)
