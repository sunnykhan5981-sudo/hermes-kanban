# LUDOLAND Facebook Video Hashtag Strategy

## Hashtag Rules (Bangladesh Market)

### Composition
- **Primary**: 3-5 high-volume Bangla hashtags
- **Secondary**: 3-5 medium-volume mix (Bangla + English)
- **Niche**: 2-3 specific gaming hashtags
- **Branded**: 1 LUDOLAND hashtag always
- **Total**: 10-15 hashtags per video (Facebook best practice)

## Pro Tips

1. **First comment strategy**: Put 5-7 hashtags in first comment to avoid clutter
2. **Location tags**: Always tag "Bangladesh" + specific city
3. **Trending tags**: Monitor #TrendingBangladesh, #BDTrending
4. **Avoid spam**: Don't use same 15 hashtags on every post — rotate
5. **Branded tag**: Create challenge: #লুডোল্যান্ডচ্যালেঞ্জ — UGC incentive
